WHAT WE ARE ABOUT

Our plan is to have two programs.

One will be a conventional space at our apartment, where we want to show predominantly young artists in a combination of solo and group shows. The apartment setting will allow for a more engaged art viewing experience, where the less formal home setting will create a more relaxed atmosphere. The art space itself will have minimal viewer interference, being as close to a white cube as possible, within the neo classical space.

The other programme will be more experimental, where we will organise temporary and interdisciplinary happenings that wonâ€™t be publicised online. Instead, we will experiment by using offline advertising forms, such as radio, street advertising, magazines, and word of mouth. The happenings will be documented in zine form, with the intention to build up a library of material that will be accessible at the apartment gallery space. The zines will also be available on the website, as it is necessary to be generous towards the artists and the public
